Rock, Paper, Scissors!

Austin Powers and Dr. Evil are at it again! This time, they are settling the score with one of the oldest games they remember - The Rock, Paper, Scissors! It is up to you to help our hero emerge victorious!

You will play a 5 round deathmatch. After 5 rounds a winner will be determined, however it is possible to end with a draw, as this is a clash of titans after all. Can you help Austin tip the scales in his favor?
